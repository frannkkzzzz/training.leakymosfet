About Us

Commitment

We strongly believe that you can solve laptop problems on your own by learning how to apply the proper laptop repair skills.

Professionalism

In many ways, theory is far more significant than experience. These are both provided by our teaching methodology.

Passion

We are passionate about our work. Let’s learn laptop repair together and share our passion for this rapidly evolving technology!

Want to learn how to quickly troubleshoot laptop? High quality repairs? Less backjobs?

Mastering the art of identifying components and testing them is an essential skill that an aspiring laptop technician must learn. Knowing how each circuit works speed up the troubleshooting process. Circuit analysis will take you to the next level and prove you are a master of board-level repair!
